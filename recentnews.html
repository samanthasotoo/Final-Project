Junior Guzman-Feliz’s

A young boy named Junior Guzman Feliz with the age of 15 was killed june 20 2018 by a Dominican Gang known as the “Trinitarios”.In The bronx ny, in front of a local store on 183rd Street and Bathgate Avenue, just around the corner from his house. There is many proof (videos went viral) where the boy was trying to save his life he jumped over the store counter scared however they saw him and dragged him outside to the front of the store where he was injured very badly with a machete,once they finished the 11+ suspects left in cars, as blood left his body he ran to the hospital which was only a block away where he stopped right in front of the hospital…. according to the doctor that attended junior the cut in the neck was the cause of his death
